This Website

When deciding to create a website as a portfolio, I intentionally chose languages, frameworks and software that I have not previously used. This was because while advertising my skills I thought, why not practise with unfamiliar tools to demonstrate my versatility.

My Decisions

I decided on designing a clear and simple UI to convey as much information as possible without overwhelming the user. Hence, not being afraid to add as many pages as possible so that the user can choose what they would like to know more about and ignore the rest. I believe this format is a more comprehensive style of CV/portfolio as it allows the user to fully be in control of what information they are looking for.
