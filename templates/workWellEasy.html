Senior Software Developer

I joined a start-up as part of a kickstart scheme. Where naturally, I became the Senior Developer since Well Easy had a team of 2 employees besides the founders.

Description

Well Easy use a Shopify website to sell heath and eco-friendly products with a focus on catering to specific lifestyles (such as Vegan, Gluten-free, Ketogenic and many more). The website has only been live for a couple of months at the start of my employment. Hence, there are many areas to work on. Initially, I have been working on the website's UI and UX. From small changes such as fixing the CTA and layout, to rebuilding small parts of the site to establish the company's personality. Eventually I will move on to building new features and rebuilding the codes structure, as Shopify does not have the most efficient structure by default.

What I Gained

Having the title Senior Developer has been a privilege to receive at such an early stage in my career. This level of authority has enabled me to provide more insight in team meetings, helping the start-up gage a better understanding of the use of technology as well as further push my experience and knowledge as a Project Manager and Software Developer. I am excited as to where this position will take me and to continue to grow.
More coming soon...
