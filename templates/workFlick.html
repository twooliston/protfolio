UI and UX Analysis

As a personal project, I worked with a new start up, called Flick Tech, and analyse their web app. Flick Tech offers software which helps users with business Instagram account utilise hashtags to maximise their Instagram posts' potential reach. This service is subscription based.

Description

This project will be looking to increase Flick's customer retention focusing on their user interface and experience. The scope only covered the core system (excluding bugs and functionality issues), once the user has logged on. Therefore, this project does not account for customers leaving due to not seeing an increase in their reach (because of misunderstanding the purpose of the software), cost/benefit issues and/or other issues. Not being an expert on their website and their reasoning of chosen design, I decided to analyse their compliance with HCI principles and current user experience and lead to suggesting appropriate improvements and additional features to employ.

What I Gained

This project took approximately 4 weeks to complete. I believe it to have been beneficial to my experience and understanding for HCI and the implementation of persuasive technology. In the future, I will be better prepared when designing and analysing interfaces. Furthermore, I have a better insight on how the project could be further expanded with the help of interviews, questionnaires, focus groups as well as more time personally spent using the system.
