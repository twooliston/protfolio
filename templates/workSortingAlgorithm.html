Sorting Algorithm Visualiser

This was a project I decided to take on to demonstrate my familiarity with sort algorithms and being able to visually demonstrate how they function as well as their efficiency.

My Decisions

I decided to work with 3 sorting algorithms (merge, quick and bubble). Each of the algorithms have their suitability in their application and therefore, was the reason why I selected to work with these algorithms. I used a colour code to show the sorting process.
- Red for showing the values currently being compared
- Turquoise for when the value had been visited for the first time.
- Cadet blue for undiscovered values.
