Bluefuse Systems

For my industrial placement, while studying at the University of Bath, I worked at Bluefuse Systems for a full year.

Description

Blufuse Systems is a small digital agency based in Bath. Their role is to build and maintain websites of varying complexity for clients. Mainly a large tax management company in New Zealand (TMNZ) which takes up to 80% of the resources. The ongoing projects and technologies, at the time, used at Bluefuse vary, from using WordPress to build blogs and websites, to using PHP and Angular 2 to build larger software systems.

What I Gained

What I found most interesting during the placement was the use of functional code, which was not distinguished during the course at the University of Bath. As my interest in coding is to closely related to maintainability, readability and efficiency, the use of functional code had a important impact on my work.
